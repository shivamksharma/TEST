Our Journey

From a simple idea to connecting billions of conversations worldwide.

2016

The Beginning

Two engineers, frustrated by unreliable SMS delivery during a major e-commerce outage, started building a more dependable messaging platform.

"What if we could make communication as reliable as the internet itself?"

2018

First Enterprise Win

Launched our first multi-country OTP infrastructure for a global bank, achieving 99.995% uptime and proving our platform's reliability at scale.
